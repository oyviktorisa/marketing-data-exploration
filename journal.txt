*Data cleaning*
Eliminated people who didn't make any purchase either via web, store, or catalogue.
Created 4 new variables :
- PurchaseMethod : to determine which method are used the most by a customer
- EducationLvl, MaritalStatus : to put the scattered values in single column (from boolean to categorical)
- AgeGroup : to group customers into 10 year range of age group
Removed the old education and marital status columns.
