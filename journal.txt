*Objective*
The objective is to analyze the campaign performance.

*Data cleaning*
Eliminated people who didn't make any purchase either via web, store, or catalogue.
Created 5 new variables :
- PurchaseMethod : to determine which method are used the most by a customer
- EducationLvl, MaritalStatus : to put the scattered values in single column (from boolean to categorical)
- AgeGroup : to group customers into 10 year range of age group
- MostPurchased : to determine what is the most purchased product by a customer
Removed the old education and marital status columns.

*Data analysis*
Divided the customer into 3 category based on the purchase method they most used
Analyzed the characteristics of each category
Analyzed the characteristics of customers that accepted campaign(s)
